Hello! My name is Whitney Tran. I am a second-year undergraduate student at the University of California, Riverside. I volunteer at the Brain Game Center as a research assistant. As a research assistant, I walk participants through studies and I also test different apps created at the lab for bugs. In terms of my research interests, I am interested in strategies to improve cognition and how those strategies can be applied to the classroom. I am also interested in teacher-to-student interactions and relationships and how it can impact student achievement.

As for post-undergraduate, I hope to continue my education in Psychology in a Master's or Doctorate program, focusing on cognition.

Outside of school, I enjoy cooking and baking. My favorite dessert to bake is egg tarts. I also enjoy trying new foods and restaurants around the area.
